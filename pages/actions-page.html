Install smart lightning controls

Automated lightning controls ensure lights are only used when they need to be, reducing your electricity consumption. The common types are:

  • Presence sensors: These will detect whether people are present and turn on light accordingly. The two most common types are desk sensors and movement sensors in the ceiling fittings.
  • Timers or daylight based: Automating lights based on daylight hours ensures light only turn on when it's dark.
